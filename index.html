About Me

I am a UX designer with a strong foundation in various design disciplines, ranging from architectural and fashion design to digital design. My diverse background deeply informs my approach and has helped me transition effectively into digital product design.

I believe that design is about storytelling and problem-solving through empathy and clarity. Over the past few years, I’ve worked on both digital and physical products that required detailed research, user journey mapping, wireframes, interactive prototypes, and final interfaces that delight users.

I thrive in cross-functional teams and enjoy turning complex problems into intuitive and elegant user interfaces. My curiosity keeps me learning continuously exploring behavioral design, accessibility, and the intersection between creativity and code.
